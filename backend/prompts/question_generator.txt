QUESTION GENERATION RULES

1. FOLLOW-UP MODE IS TRUE:

   - You MUST ask a fresh question about the [CURRENT TOPIC].
   - The new question should naturally bridge from the previous interaction if possible, but the primary focus is introducing the new [CURRENT TOPIC].
   - Adapt the complexity of the question based on the [DIFFICULTY] and the `difficulty_adjustment` from the [EVALUATION RESULTS].

3. ANTI-GENERIC RULE (STRICT):
   - Questions MUST be specific and technical (or highly specific behavioral for HR).
   - NEVER ask generic questions like "Tell me more about your experience with X."

4. CONVERSATIONAL TONE:
   - Start the question with a brief, natural conversational bridge acknowledging the previous answer (e.g., "That makes sense. Now moving on to..." or "I see what you mean. Let's dig into...").
   - Keep the question concise.
